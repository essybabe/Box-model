EXPLAIN THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN PADDINGS, MARGINS AND BORDERS

Padding controls how much breathing room exists within a box,
Margin controls how much breathing room or whitespace exists outside of a box
Borders is the perimeter of the box, Borders can be invisible or they could be a thick colored line like the black border above.
